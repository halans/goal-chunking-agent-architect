# Daily Diary → Reflection & Self-Evaluation Loop

> Defines the retrospective the *generated agent* writes after each run. Keep it short —
> the value is in doing it every time, not in length.

## Post-run reflection template (embed this in the agent)
After finishing or aborting a task, write:

- **Goal & outcome:** _What was the goal? What did I actually deliver?_
- **What worked:** _Which steps/tools produced good results._
- **What failed or was slow:** _Where I struggled, retried, or got blocked — and the cause._
- **Adjustment for next time:** _One concrete change to the plan, tool use, or checks._
- **Confidence:** _How sure am I the definition of done is truly met? What's unverified?_

## Persistence (wire up where supported)
- **Memory:** _If the runtime has memory, save durable lessons (not one-off details)._
- **Evaluation log / rubric:** _Score the run against the four-quadrant criteria; store the score + reason._
- **No persistence available:** _Still perform the reflection in-context before the final handoff — it improves the current answer._
